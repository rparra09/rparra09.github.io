01 · About

Trust is built, not assumed.

Fifteen years of turning high-stakes data into something organizations can rely on.

I'm a data management professional with 15+ years across biotech, financial services, and enterprise technology. Today I'm a Data Manager at Amgen, after leading data management at Gilead Sciences and building a foundation in data analysis, SAP security, and data services.

My focus is the intersection of data governance and responsible AI: the quality foundations of ISO 8000, the supply-chain data standards of ISO 25500, and the AI governance practices that frameworks like the EU AI Act and ISO/IEC 22989:2023 now demand. I certify what I practice: from AI security and governance to master data quality.

Trustworthy AI starts with high-quality, trusted data. This transparency is what turns compliance into confidence.
